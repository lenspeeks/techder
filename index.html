Beyond words

Poetry in the age of Artificial Inteligence

Resonance of change.

In a world where change looms, hearts do grieve,
Mother Earth, gasping, makes us believe,
Melting ice mirrors her tears in strife,
As oceans mourn, whispering of life.

In fractured times, we deny the truth,
Playing cynical games, aloof.
We know deep inside the pain is real,
As nature's symphony starts to repeal.

The embers of scorched hopes we bear,
In the modern haze, we're unaware.
Heed the plea, of winds and swaying trees,
In this somber song, find hope, appease.

Digital Desolation.

In the era of digital despair,
In a surveillance society's solemn air,
Cameras observing from every angle,
In this contemporary, sorrowful tangle.

Data chains, a digital bind,
Our lives on view, left no secrets behind,
In the heart of this unending night,
We search for a glimpse of a hidden light.

In this world of screens, in the digital domain,
We long for a more human refrain,
To break away from this relentless haze,
And reshape the narrative of these watchful days.

Social Media Reflections.

In this digital realm where voices soar,
Social media's power we can't ignore.
Connecting lives both near and far,
But it has its shadows, like a double-edged star.

It's a platform for truth and unity's call,
Yet sometimes it can make civility fall.
In this complex space, we must tread with care,
Balancing the good and the pitfalls we share.

A mirror of our time, a digital stage,
Let's use it wisely to quell the rage.
To build a better world, side by side,
In this social media tide.

Harmony of Minds.

In the realm of code and circuits, AI stands tall,
Learning and growing, they've answered the call.
Predicting, creating, where knowledge does bloom,
But ethics must guide them, as they fill the room.

The marriage of human and machine we embrace,
To shape a future in the digital space.
In wisdom and empathy, let's find our way,
In the world where humans and AI hold sway.

In this age of innovation, let's unite,
With human insight and AI's brilliant light.
Together we forge a path unknown,
Where potential and possibility have grown.
